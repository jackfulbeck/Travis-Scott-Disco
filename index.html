4. Birds in the Trap Sing McKnight

Travis Scott’s “Birds in the Trap Sing McKnight” album cover, https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Birds_in_the_Trap_Sing_McKnight

Bittsm is ranked in last place because it just isn’t memorable for me, it does have hits like “goosebumps”, I just don’t find myself coming back to it.

3. Utopia

Travis Scott’s “Utopia” album cover, https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utopia_%28Travis_Scott_album%29

Utopia is ranked 3rd. It has a definite aesthetic, and has its hits as well. I just don’t think it’s as good as the higher one's on the list.

2. Rodeo

Travis Scott’s “Rodeo” album cover, https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rodeo_%28Travis_Scott_album%29

Rodeo takes second here, I think the story behind it of Travis’ life into stardom and the partylife is very interesting. Even as a debut album, I really like it. This was very close to first place.

1. Astroworld

Travis Scott’s “Astroworld” album cover, https://www.amazon.com/ASTROWORLD-Travis-Scott/dp/B07FYQR5QQ

I have to give it up to Astroworld for the win here. I come back to it so often, every song on this album is worth listening to, and it just sounds so good.
